== Description ==

LeadOutcome is a revolutionary new system that is specifically designed to significantly grow your business and your team.  LeadOutcome provides you with the guidance and tools to generate, nurture, and convert leads into customers.  You will quickly and easily increase the size of your team, customer base, and your income.

Your started your company to earn a significant income - this marketing system is the vehicle that will help you get there.  Not only does it replace to have multiple costly systems, but it also provides you with all the marketing tools a business needs, in a completely integrated system.  You will find many features not found in any other online marketing system.  LeadOutcome will help you grow, manage, and explode your business.

== Installation ==

<ol>
<li>Install the LeadOutcome WordPress plugin into your WordPress site.  You can accomplish this by going to the WordPress plugin search facility and search for the term LeadOutcome.  Make sure to activate the plugin</li>
<li>Enable Tracking/Scoring feature for all web pages, posts or both.</li>
<li>If you don't already have one, create a LeadOutcome Account.  (Sign Up)</li>
<li>Generate an Opt-In form from one of our professional templates using our Opt-In Form Wizard.  The wizard can be located by going to "Marketing Sites &raquo; Settings &raquo; External Web Sites &raquo; Opt-In Form Wizard".  Generate your form and copy it to the paste board.</li>
<li>Switch to your WordPress site and Select "LeadOutcome &raquo; Opt-In Form" menu</li>
<li>Add a new opt-in form and paste your generated form into the text field and save.</li>
<li>Copy and paste the shortcode into the page or post you would like to see the Opt-In form on.</li>
</ol>
